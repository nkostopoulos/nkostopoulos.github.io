Bio

I hold a diploma in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the National Technical University of Athens (NTUA). I am currently a Ph.D. student at NTUA focusing on network security tailored to the DNS service. My research interests include: computer networks, network monitoring, Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) attack detection and mitigation, probabilistic data structures, machine/deep learning and botnet traffic detection (Domain Generation Algorithms, DGAs). My supervisor is Vasilis Maglaris, Professor Emeritus at NTUA.
