Bio

I hold a diploma in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the National Technical University of Athens (NTUA) and the Engineering Ph.D. degree from NTUA. As a Ph.D. student at NTUA, I focused on network security emphasizing on the DNS service under the supervision of Prof. Emeritus Vasilis Maglaris. I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at the NETMODE laboratory of NTUA. My research interests include: computer networks, network monitoring, Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) attack detection and mitigation, probabilistic data structures, machine/deep learning and botnet traffic detection (Domain Generation Algorithms, DGAs).
